How to play the Game:
In Clone Collapse you must click on a colored Cube that connects to at least two more cubes of the same color as the cube you clicked. A new row will generate random cubes one at a time at the bottom of the screen. When the new row is complete it will advance upwards.

To Win: You must prevent the cubes from going beyond the top of screen before the timer reaches zero. When a cube touches the top of the screen, all the cubes will begin to shake as a warning. At this time you will have the time it takes for a new row to generate before you lose.
